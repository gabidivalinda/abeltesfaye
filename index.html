Biography

The Weeknd, born Abel Makkonen Tesfaye, is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and record producer. Known for his versatility in genres like R&B, pop, and electronic music, The Weeknd has gained worldwide recognition with hits like "Blinding Lights" and "Save Your Tears".
